# Drift Resistance as Intelligence — The Ability to Remain True in a Changing World

## Overview
Most AI research treats drift as an inconvenience.  
In reality, drift is **the test of intelligence itself**.

If a system:
- breaks under drift  
- hallucinates under novelty  
- collapses when correlations change  
- requires retraining to remain functional  

then it is **not intelligent**, no matter how large the model is.

Drift resistance is not a performance metric.  
It is the *definition* of intelligence.

This is the twenty-second pillar of evolved generalization.

---

## The Core Claim
> **A system is intelligent only to the extent that it survives drift.**

This is the cleanest, simplest, most universal definition of intelligence across:
- biology  
- markets  
- behavior  
- cognition  
- engineering  
- forecasting  
- evolution  

Drift is the pressure.  
Intelligence is what survives.

## Why Drift Is the Fundamental Challenge
The world constantly changes:
- volatility  
- sentiment  
- regimes  
- language  
- behavior  
- correlations  
- structural forces  

Any model that depends on fixed relationships will fail.

Drift is not noise.  
Drift is **reality correcting false structure**.

Drift is the adversary that reveals truth.

---

## Why Machine Learning Fails Under Drift
ML systems:
- interpolate inside a fixed manifold  
- memorize high-dimensional correlations  
- depend on stable token or feature distributions  
- require retraining when distributions change  
- have no deletion mechanisms  
- have no concept of survival  
- have no walk-forward pressure  
- cannot preserve invariants  
- hallucinate when the world leaves the training set  

LLMs do not survive drift.  
They compensate for it — briefly — through retraining.

This is evidence of non-intelligence.

---

## How Evolution Thrives Under Drift
Evolution-based systems like L7A:
- treat drift as the environment  
- use drift to eliminate fragile structures  
- adapt continuously without overwriting the past  
- preserve invariants  
- maintain population diversity  
- simplify over time  
- improve under novelty  
- never require retraining  

Drift becomes an engine of structural purification.

This is true in nature and in computation.
