# Truth Alignment — Why Evolution Naturally Aligns Intelligent Systems

## Overview
In most AI architectures, *alignment* is an external process:
- RLHF  
- supervised fine-tuning  
- prompt engineering  
- adversarial training  
- guardrail systems  

These approaches bolt alignment onto a system that does not naturally align itself.

Evolution-based intelligence is fundamentally different.

**It aligns to truth automatically**, because truth is the only thing that survives the evolutionary filter.

This is the forty-ninth pillar of evolved generalization.

---

## The Core Insight
> **Evolution does not optimize for human approval.  
> It optimizes for survival under truth-bearing conditions.**

Truth becomes a structural requirement, not an overlay.

Any representation that deviates from truth:
- dies  
- simplifies  
- collapses  
- gets eliminated  

The result is an intelligence system whose internal geometry is shaped by truth, not by retrospective fitting.

---

## Why ML Requires External Alignment
Machine learning systems must be aligned externally because:
1. **They predict tokens, not truth.**  
2. **They assign probability mass even when evidence is absent.**  
3. **They cannot abstain.**  
4. **They hallucinate under uncertainty.**  
5. **They drift as embeddings drift.**  
6. **They mistake correlation for causation.**  
7. **They memorize fragile structure.**  

Alignment becomes:
- expensive  
- fragile  
- adversarial  
- continuous  
- incomplete  

Because the underlying architecture never internalizes truth.

---

## Why Evolution Aligns Itself
Evolution operates under forward pressure:
- unstable structures die  
- fragile correlations collapse  
- noise-sensing agents vanish  
- overconfident guessers are eliminated  
- geometry simplifies  
- long-term invariants accumulate  

This naturally drives systems toward:
- calibration  
- abstention in low-signal regions  
- signal integrity  
- drift resistance  
- objective consistency  
- truthful representation of structure  

Truth alignment is a *by-product of survival*.

## Why AGI Requires Truth Alignment
A safe and stable AGI must:
- avoid hallucination  
- maintain calibration  
- preserve internal geometry  
- resist drift  
- abstain under uncertainty  
- adapt through elimination
